FOCUSA v2.0 ABOUT

Welcome to the FOCUSA v2.0 help page...

Actually, this app s perfectly simple to use, though sometimes the bugs in this app can cause great problems.
So below is a comprehensive guide to solve probable problems in this app...

The dashboard[#]


Basically, the main screen is the dashboard...
It just displays the time, and almost everything important in this app. Simply put, the dashboard is like the Desktop of Windows.



The Settings pane[#]


The settings pane is the first thing you will see on starting this app.
You will have to fill the required things in, and the app will work accordingly...
You just have to follow the instructions given in the pane, and after finishing, press the 'OK' button...



Page title billboard[#]


After pressing the 'OK' button in the settings pane, you might notice nothing special happening,
until maybe a few minutes, when you will notice the page title changing very often!!
